About

A scholar across five countries.

Hana Milanov is a Professor of Entrepreneurship at IMD in Lausanne. With experience spanning five countries, she combines academic rigor, entrepreneurial insight, and a genuinely global perspective on how new ventures come to life.

Her research — published in FT50 journals and featured in outlets including Forbes — examines how entrepreneurs recognize and develop opportunities, mobilize resources, and navigate the human dimensions of venture creation, including the role of identity, teams, and career context in shaping entrepreneurial decisions and outcomes.

Before IMD, she spent over a decade at the Technical University of Munich, serving as Senior Vice-President for International Alliances and Alumni and as Academic Director of the FT #1-ranked EMBA in Innovation and Business Creation.
