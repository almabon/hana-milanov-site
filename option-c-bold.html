About
A global perspective, earned across five countries.

Hana Milanov is a Professor of Entrepreneurship at IMD in Lausanne, combining academic rigor, entrepreneurial insight, and a genuinely global view of how ventures come to life.

Her research examines how entrepreneurs recognize and develop opportunities, mobilize resources, and navigate the human dimensions of venture creation — the role of identity, teams, and career context in shaping entrepreneurial decisions and outcomes. Before IMD she spent over a decade at the Technical University of Munich, where she was Senior Vice-President for International Alliances and Academic Director of the FT #1-ranked EMBA in Innovation & Business Creation.
